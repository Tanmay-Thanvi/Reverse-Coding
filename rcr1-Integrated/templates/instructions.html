INSTRUCTIONS

1. The competition is of 30 minutes.

2. The question put in front of you has only ONE INTEGER answer.

3. You will only proceed if you attempt the question.

4. You will have 2 attempts to answer the question.

5. The attempts will carry marks given in the marking scheme i.e.
the first attempt will have a marking scheme of +4,-2 and the second attempt has marking scheme of +2,-1.

6. You can log out of the competition any time you want through the LOGOUT button.

7. You will be given 2 LIFELINES which are explained below.

1
Life Line 1

Zones of destiny

The players will be divided into 3 zones according to their current score in the contest.
Zone 1 - Red Zone
Zone 2 - Green Zone
Zone 3 - Purple Zone

The color of the progress bar denotes the zone of the particular player.

By means of this lifeline, a player can change the marking scheme he/she plays with for 1 question as follows:
Red Zone – +4,-2 (no change)
Green Zone – +6,-4
Purple Zone – +10,-10

Note that this lifeline may be used only once and the marking scheme shall remain the same for both attempts.

2
Life Line 2

Cheatcode

When you enter the green Zone, you are eligible to use this lifeline You can activate this life only for one particular question.
By activating this lifeline your current question will be replaced by a riddle with the marking scheme + 2 for a correct answer and -4 for a wrong one.

Note -- The format of answer will be 'all small no spaces'.
